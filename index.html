    IndustryComplete Support Centre

    Welcome to the IndustryComplete Support Centre. Here you'll find everything you need to install, configure, and get the most out of IndustryComplete — the AI-driven industry classification app for Salesforce by ProvenWorks.

    IndustryComplete automatically retrieves company information, assigns recognised NAICS, SIC, and ISIC industry classifications, and maps them to your custom Salesforce® Industry field values — all within your existing workflows.

    What is IndustryComplete?

    IndustryComplete is a Salesforce AppExchange app that helps you classify your Accounts and Leads with industry codes from leading standards:

    • SIC – Standard Industrial Classification
    • ISIC – International Standard Industrial Classification
    • NAICS – North American Industry Classification System

    Key capabilities include:

    • One-click AI enrichment – Use AI to enrich Accounts and Leads with industry classifications and company descriptions directly within Salesforce.
    • Automatic field mapping – Assign industry codes and map them to your custom Salesforce Industry picklist values for consistent, structured data.
    • Flow automation – Automate enrichment at scale using Salesforce Flow, without requiring user interaction.
    • Live company data – Retrieve up-to-date company information at the time of enrichment to avoid reliance on stale records.
    • Improved reporting – Use structured industry data to filter, segment, and analyse your prospects and customers more effectively.
